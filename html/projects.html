Antigravity Hand Tracking

This is a Python-based real-time hand-tracking tool that focuses on smooth, aesthetic visuals. It solves the problem of accurately tracking hand movements in real-time for immersive applications. It utilizes MediaPipe and OpenCV to capture and process inputs, generating glowing connections between corresponding fingertips of both hands in real-time.
