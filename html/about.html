About Me

I am Jhon Paul Baonil, an aspiring web developer and virtual assistant focused on helping small businesses and coaches establish a strong and effective online presence. I specialize in building websites using platforms like WordPress and Wix, with an emphasis on clean design, functionality, and user experience.

Although I am at the early stage of my professional journey, I have developed a solid foundation through hands-on experience during my work immersion, where I worked on coding tasks, data entry, and assisted in teaching basic computer skills. This experience strengthened my attention to detail, adaptability, and ability to learn quickly in a practical environment.

In addition to web development, I offer support in administrative tasks, social media management, scheduling, and data organization. I prefer working on non-voice tasks where I can deliver consistent, high-quality output with efficiency and accuracy.

I am currently focused on building my portfolio, gaining real-world experience, and working with clients who value reliability, clear communication, and long-term growth. My goal is to continuously improve my technical skills while delivering solutions that provide real value to businesses.
